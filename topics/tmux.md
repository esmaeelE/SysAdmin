# Tmux 


## Cheat sheet

### Kill

```
Ctrl+B: kill-session – kill the active session, all its windows and panes                                                                                                    
Ctrl+B: kill-window or Ctrl+B & – kill the active window and all panes within it                                                                                             
Ctrl+B: kill-pane or Ctrl+B x – kill the active pane
```
